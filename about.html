My Journey

Originally from San Francisco, I moved to Cork, Ireland to pursue my passion for computer science. I started out with full-stack development, building web applications with React and Flask. As I progressed through my degree at UCC, I discovered a deep interest in algorithms, game development, and systems programming, working with C and low-level networking. This eventually led me to machine learning, where I can combine my skills with data science..

Now back in San Francisco with my Upper Second-Class Honors degree, I'm strengthening my technical foundation while actively seeking internship opportunities. My goal is to become a software engineer where I can apply this diverse skill set to solve complex problems.
